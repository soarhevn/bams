輔助說明: 增加繳款記錄 - Insert Pro Rata Income

Enter the date that the various dues begin on, usually the date the member joins. If the member is marked as a monthly pay member, the remaining months in the half year the of date choosen will be calculated.

Even if the member is not new, 'new member dues' will be generated. Once the dues are created for this time period, simply 'edit' the dues to delete the 'new member dues'.
