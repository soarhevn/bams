輔助說明: 編輯會員繳款記錄 - Edit Member Income Record

Edit the dues for this member. CAREFUL - changing an amount after it has been paid will change the monthly income total!

  • Month: only use this if member is paying by month and not every six months.
  • Dues amounts: do not use commas when entering the amounts.
  • Wire: if dues were wired in, select the account the dues were wired into. This will allow the system to calculate how much money to transfer to each account for wired dues at the end of each month.

New Member Entry Dues

IMPORTANT: Marking dues as paid on this page will not show the new member entry dues as paid also! You must add a transaction under 其它事務處理 and manually log the amount paid from 經常會費 account receivable to the selected bank account it was paid into.
