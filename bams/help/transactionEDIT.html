輔助說明: 編輯帳目 - Edit Transaction

科目 / 抵消科目

Pick the primary account that is to be debited or credited on the left, then on the right pick the cross account that is affected.

借 - Debit / 貸 - Credit

Do not fill in both fields, fill only the amount for debt or credit for the primary account that was picked on the left side. The cross account amount will be filled in automatically by the system.

摘要

Notes must be entered for the tranaction or the system will generate an error.
