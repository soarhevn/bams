輔助說明: 會員搜尋 - Member Search

Member search gives you the ability to search for members several different ways and to narrow your search results. You can type in any amount of text in the search fields, even a partial name or number; any results with that section will be returned. More items filled in will narrow the results.

  • Card number will search on member card number, to select all of the card numbers starting with "J", type "J" and cards starting with "J" will be returned.
  • ID Number search will search for a full or partial number.
  • Name: search by any character in the name.
  • Salary: select the salary level desired.
  • Medical and labor insurance can be selected as searching for members with and without insurance, or those just with, or those with none.
  • Check the inactive members box to include inactive members in your search.
